Common Questions

What does it cost to become a partner?

Nothing upfront. Helacore partnerships are free to join. We earn when you earn. You receive a percentage of revenue generated from users you bring to the platform—there is no cost or risk to you at any point.

How is revenue sharing calculated?

When a user signs up through your partner link or API key and subscribes to a paid plan, you receive 30-40% of that subscription revenue (tier depends on volume and integration depth). Payments are made monthly via bank transfer or M-Pesa with full transparency through your partner dashboard.

How long does onboarding take?

Simple referral partnerships can be live in 48 hours. API integrations typically take 2-4 weeks. White-label deployments take 4-8 weeks depending on customization requirements. Every partner gets a dedicated integration engineer throughout.

Can we white-label Helacore?

Yes, white-label is available to Enterprise-tier partners. You can deploy Helacore under your own brand, custom domain, and feature set. We handle all infrastructure, updates, and maintenance while your users see only your brand. Additional customization fees may apply depending on scope.

Do you partner with organizations outside Kenya?

Yes. We are actively expanding across East Africa (Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda) and have pilot partnerships in Nigeria, Ghana, and South Africa. If your organization operates in any African market, we want to talk.

What's the minimum user volume?

There is no minimum volume for referral partnerships. For API and white-label integrations, we recommend a pilot of at least 100 users to validate the model before scaling. Revenue share percentages increase at higher volume tiers.
