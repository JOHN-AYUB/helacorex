Community Guidelines

We keep the community safe, respectful, and valuable. These guidelines ensure every member has a positive experience.

Be Respectful

Treat every member with respect regardless of their experience level, education level, or business size. No question is too basic.

Be Helpful

Prioritize being useful. Share specific, actionable advice based on real experience rather than generic advice.

Be Constructive

Frame feedback as "I tried X, it helped me with Y" rather than "That doesn't work." Give context and share what you actually did.

Be Inclusive

Use language that welcomes everyone—English, Swahili, Sheng, Kikuyu, Luganda, Dholuo. Avoid jargon that excludes.

Report Problems

If you see harmful, misleading, or spam content, report it. We handle reports within 4 hours.

Share Context

Always include your business type, location, and relevant details. Context helps others understand if advice applies to them.
