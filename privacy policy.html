Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 24, 2024

Your business data is your most valuable asset. This policy outlines exactly how we protect it, process it, and ensure your privacy remains absolute while using our AI ecosystem.

1. Introduction

Helacore Inc. ("Helacore," "we," "us," or "our") operates the AI-powered business guidance platform available at helacore.africa and associated mobile applications (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

We operate under the principles of data minimization and privacy-by-design. Given the sensitive nature of financial and business data processed on our platform, we hold ourselves to standards that meet or exceed the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, and international best practices such as the GDPR.

By using the Service, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, information collected automatically when you use the Service, and information from third-party sources.

  • Account Data: Name, email address, phone number, business name, and business type provided during registration.
  • Business Data: Sales records, expense logs, inventory levels, loan details, and cash flow data you input or connect via integrations (e.g., M-Pesa).
  • Voice Data: Audio inputs processed via the Voice Interface for intent parsing. Audio is processed ephemerally and not stored persistently.
  • Usage Data: Log data, device information, IP address, browser type, operating system, and interaction patterns with the platform.
  • Marketplace Data: Transaction records and communications facilitated through the Marketplace Connector.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to operate, maintain, and improve the Service, including:

  • To calculate your Financial Health Score and provide actionable AI recommendations.
  • To run What-If Simulations tailored to your specific business context.
  • To facilitate connections between you and third-party service providers (credit, suppliers) via the Marketplace.
  • To communicate with you regarding product updates, security alerts, and support inquiries.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and prevent fraudulent or illegal activity.

4. Federated Learning & AI Privacy

YOUR RAW BUSINESS DATA DOES NOT LEAVE YOUR DEVICE.

Helacore utilizes a Federated Learning architecture to train our global AI models. This means:

  • The AI model is downloaded to your local device (phone or browser).
  • The model learns from your business data locally, on your hardware.
  • Only the mathematical "weight updates" (how the model should adjust its parameters)—not your raw data—are sent to our secure servers.
  • We apply Secure Aggregation and Differential Privacy to these updates, ensuring that your specific data points cannot be reverse-engineered from the model updates.

This approach ensures that the Market Intelligence Network gets smarter for everyone, while your individual sales, expenses, and customer data remain strictly private on your device.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information or raw business data to third parties. We may share generic, anonymized, or aggregated information with third parties for the purposes outlined below:

  • Service Providers: We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service (e.g., cloud hosting, M-Pesa integration APIs). These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
  • Marketplace Connectors: If you explicitly opt-in to connect with a credit provider or supplier, we will share the minimum necessary data (e.g., Health Score, verification status) to facilitate that introduction.
  • Legal Requirements: Helacore may disclose your Personal Data in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect the rights or property of Helacore, or act in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users or the public.

6. Data Security

The security of your data is critical to us. We implement a zero-trust security architecture and industry-standard measures, including:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3.
  • On-Device Processing: Federated Learning ensures raw data remains on your device behind your personal device security (biometrics/PIN).
  • Access Controls: Strict internal access controls ensure our own engineers cannot view your raw business data.
  • Regular Audits: We undergo regular third-party security audits and penetration testing.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Data Retention

Helacore will retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will retain and use your Personal Data to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our legal agreements.

For business data, we retain active data on our servers for the duration your account is active. Data used for AI model training via Federated Learning is processed ephemerally and is not retained on central servers.

8. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • The right to rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure: You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability: You have the right to request the transfer of your data to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

You can exercise these rights by navigating to Account Settings within the app or by contacting us at privacy@helacore.africa.

9. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Service and we hold certain information.

Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Service.

Examples of Cookies we use:

  • Strictly Necessary: Required for the Service to function (e.g., authentication session).
  • Performance: To understand how users interact with the Service (anonymized).
  • Functionality: To remember your preferences (e.g., Light/Dark mode).

10. Changes & Contact Information

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
