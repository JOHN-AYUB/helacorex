Bridging the Information Gap

In emerging markets, the difference between a thriving business and a failing one isn't effort—it's access to timely, actionable intelligence. Helacore democratizes this access.

Capital Preservation

Most micro-businesses operate on razor-thin margins. A single bad inventory decision can wipe out a month's savings. Helacore's predictive models prevent capital leakage by optimizing stock levels and timing expenditures to market rhythms.

  • Reduced dead stock by 45%
  • Optimized M-Pesa float balances
  • Prevented loan default cascades

Revenue Acceleration

Knowing what to sell, when to sell it, and at what price is traditionally reserved for corporations with data teams. Our Causal AI engine brings this power to street vendors, identifying hidden demand patterns and pricing opportunities.

  • Dynamic pricing suggestions
  • Peak demand forecasting
  • Geographic expansion signals

Community Resilience

When one business stabilizes, it creates a ripple effect—suppliers get paid, families eat, local economies circulate capital. Federated Learning ensures the entire ecosystem gets smarter without compromising individual privacy.
